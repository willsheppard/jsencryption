FAQ

  1. Is this secure?

    Yes, the encryption used is very secure.

  2. What kind of encryption is used?

    256-bit AES that is compatible with OpenSSL using a modified version of Gibberish AES.

  3. Is there a back door? Can you read my encrypted text?

    No. No one without the key can read the encrypted text and I don't know how to make a good back door.

  4. Is this going to install bad stuff on my computer or break stuff? Is this adware crap? Is this a virus?

    No. I don't know how to do that.

  5. I lost the key, can you help me decrypt the text?

    No. You lose the key and there's pretty much no way to decrypt the text, that is, until we get quantum computers.

  6. Do you have a quantum computer?

    Yes, but I don't know how to use it.
