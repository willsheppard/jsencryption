About VincentCheung.ca

Mission statement: To provide useful tools and knowledge to simplify and improve the lives of others

When I find something frustrating, lacking, or inefficient, I often write a program to automate the task. I also like working on cool and fun projects. If I think others would also benefit from it, then I put it up here.

I studied at the University of Toronto for my PhD in Computer Engineering, did my undergraduate in Computer Engineering at the University of Manitoba, and have previously worked at Microsoft Research and Google. I've won a bunch of entrepreneurship and academic awards, a full listing of which, you can find if you use the stalking aids below.
